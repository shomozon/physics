2.第三法則

\begin{eqnarray} e = \dfrac{h^2A}{GM}, \ p = \dfrac{h^2}{GM} とする。楕円の時、0 < e < 1\\ r = \dfrac{\dfrac{h^2}{GM}}{1 + \dfrac{h^2A}{GM}\cos (\theta - \theta_0)} \Leftrightarrow r\left( 1 + \dfrac{h^2A}{GM}\cos (\theta - \theta_0) \right) = \dfrac{h^2}{GM} \\ \Leftrightarrow r + \dfrac{h^2A}{GM}r\cos (\theta - \theta_0) = \dfrac{h^2}{GM} \Leftrightarrow r + \dfrac{h^2A}{GM}x = \dfrac{h^2}{GM} \Leftrightarrow r = \dfrac{h^2}{GM} - \dfrac{h^2A}{GM}x \\ \Leftrightarrow r^2 = \left(\dfrac{h^2}{GM} - \dfrac{h^2A}{GM}x \right)^2 \Leftrightarrow x^2 + y^2 = \left(\dfrac{h^2}{GM} - \dfrac{h^2A}{GM}x \right)^2 \Leftrightarrow x^2 + y^2 = \left(p - ex \right)^2 \\ \Leftrightarrow x^2 + y^2 = p^2 - 2pex + (ex)^2 \Leftrightarrow \left( 1 - e^2 \right)x^2 + 2pex + y^2 = p^2 \\ \left( 1 - e^2 \right)x^2 + 2pex + y^2 = p^2 \Leftrightarrow \left( 1 - e^2 \right)\left( x + \dfrac{pe}{1 - e^2} \right)^2 + y^2 = p^2 + \dfrac{(pe)^2}{1 - e^2} \\ \Leftrightarrow \dfrac{\left( x + \dfrac{pe}{1 - e^2} \right)^2}{\dfrac{p^2 + \dfrac{(pe)^2}{1 - e^2}}{1 - e^2}} + \dfrac{y^2}{p^2 + \dfrac{(pe)^2}{1 - e^2}} = 1 \\ \Leftrightarrow \dfrac{\left( x + \dfrac{pe}{1 - e^2} \right)^2}{\dfrac{p^2}{(1 - e^2)^2}} + \dfrac{y^2}{\dfrac{p^2}{1 - e^2}} = 1 \\ 楕円の面積S = \pi \cdot \dfrac{p}{1 - e^2} \cdot \dfrac{p}{\sqrt{1 - e^2}} = \dfrac{\pi p^2}{(1 - e^2)^\frac{3}{2}} \\ 周期T = \dfrac{S}{\frac{h}{2}} = \dfrac{2\pi}{\sqrt{GM}}a^\frac{3}{2} \end{eqnarray}
